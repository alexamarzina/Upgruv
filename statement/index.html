Essentially, there are a whole bunch of things that get Gen Z's attention, but you have to catch them before they fade. Fads are only cool for so long until the next big thing comes around (like how 13 Reasons Why is overlooking Shameless on Netflix for the time being.



Knowing what is trending and popular currently can be difficult, and that's part of the reason why teens/twenty-somethings find it all entertaining; their interest doesn't have to be maintained for long. Gone are the days of everyone liking the same thing at once for a long time. Now, everyone wants to be unique, so trends are quite fleeting in today's world.
